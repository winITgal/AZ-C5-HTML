In HTML, attributes are additional pieces of information or properties that can be added to HTML elements to modify their behavior, appearance, or provide metadata. Attributes provide essential details and instructions to web browsers and web developers. They can define characteristics such as source URLs for images, links to other web pages, and alternative text for images. Attributes play a crucial role in enhancing the functionality and presentation of web content and are an integral part ofcreating structured and interactive web pages .
